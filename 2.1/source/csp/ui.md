##Parameter Notes
### Clipboard
Specify whether to allow the use of the clipboard.

* Do not change
* Enable
* Disable

### Clear Clipboard?
This check box specifies whether to clear the clipboard.

* Enable: Clear the clipboard.
* Disable: Don't clear the clipboard.
